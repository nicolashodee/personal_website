Bienvenue sur ma galerie en ligne !

Passionné de photographie depuis plus de 10 ans, je photographie essentiellement les Alpes et les paysages que je rencontre en voyage.
Diplomé en 2015 de l'école des Gobelins à Paris, j'accorde une importance particulière à la couleur et à la composition, notamment au travers de clairs-obscurs fugaces ou de journées glacées passées sur les sommets.

C'est une quête perpétuelle, une invitation à admirer les splendeurs de la Nature, et dont j'espère ne jamais me lasser.
J'espère que vous apprécierez ces quelques instants partagés !

 C'est par ici !

Prints & Downloads

All the photos on this website have been shot using a Canon EOS 6D combined with a f4 L IS 24-105mm and a Canon EF f1.4 50mm USM lenses.
This gear allow most my photos to be printed up to 55-60cm (greatest dimension) without suffering a loss of resolution and therefore quality (the standard for photographic prints being 240pixels per inch). For bigger sizes or for cropped images, please contact me directly. >> More informations here

Print orders

To order prints, you can click on the 'buy print' link at the bottom of any photo in fullscreen view. Print services is handled with Fotomoto and the quality and packaging is very good. They ship to pretty much all over the world.

Licensing and usage
For all inquiries including licensing images for commercial, editorial, personal, stock, or photography assignment please use the buy button at the bottom of any photo (in full screen view) with your request and I will get back to you as soon as possible.

Personal usage
You can use my photos for personal use like desktop wallpapers, but for any commercial use please contact me to discuss licensing options. Also if you want to use my photos in your blog, instagram, facebook, etc. as a background or decoration please contact me first and let me know.
